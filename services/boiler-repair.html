Boiler repair & servicing in Manchester

A broken boiler is more than an inconvenience — in cold weather it can be a genuine emergency. Our Gas Safe registered engineers carry out boiler repairs across the whole of Greater Manchester, with same-day appointments available for urgent callouts. We work on all major brands and can usually diagnose a fault within the first visit.

All repairs are carried out to Gas Safe standards. You'll receive a written quote before we start any work, and we only fit genuine or manufacturer-approved parts.

Common boiler faults we fix

  • No heating or hot water — thermostat, diverter valve, or pump failure
  • Boiler showing a fault code or lockout — we diagnose and reset
  • Low boiler pressure — pressure loss, failed expansion vessel, or leaking PRV
  • Boiler leaking water — corroded heat exchanger, failed seals, or loose joints
  • Boiler making banging, whistling, or kettling sounds
  • Pilot light keeps going out — faulty thermocouple or gas valve
  • Radiators not heating properly — pump failure or system imbalance

Annual boiler servicing

An annual boiler service is the single most effective way to extend the life of your boiler, maintain your manufacturer warranty, and keep your energy bills down. During a service we clean the heat exchanger and burner, check all seals and connections, test the flue for carbon monoxide, and provide a full written report.

If you're a landlord, we also issue CP12 Gas Safety Certificates as required by law for all rental properties in England and Wales.
