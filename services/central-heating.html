Central heating services in Manchester

An inefficient central heating system costs you more to run and puts extra strain on your boiler. Whether you have cold radiators, an unbalanced system, or you're adding a new room to your heating circuit, our Gas Safe engineers carry out the full range of central heating work across Greater Manchester.

Power flushing

If your radiators have cold spots, your boiler is noisy, or your system takes a long time to heat up, sludge and rust deposits in the system are likely responsible. A power flush uses high-velocity, chemically-treated water to remove this buildup, restoring heating efficiency and protecting the boiler heat exchanger from damage.

We typically recommend fitting a magnetic system filter at the same time to catch any future debris before it accumulates. A power flush for a typical 3-bed house takes around half a day.

Smart heating controls

Upgrading to a smart thermostat such as a Nest, Hive, or Tado can reduce heating bills by up to 30% by learning your routine and only heating when needed. We supply and fit all major brands and can integrate smart controls with your existing system or a new boiler installation.
