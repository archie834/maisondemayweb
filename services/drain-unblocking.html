Drain unblocking in Manchester

A blocked drain can quickly become a health hazard if left untreated. Whether it's a slow-draining kitchen sink, a completely blocked outside drain, or sewage backing up through a toilet, our engineers carry the equipment to clear it — usually on the same visit.

We use professional-grade high-pressure water jetting equipment to clear blockages completely, not just temporarily. Unlike manual rodding, jetting cleans the inside of the pipe wall, removing grease, scale, and debris that would otherwise cause repeat blockages.

What we unblock

  • Kitchen sink and waste pipe blockages — grease and food buildup
  • Bathroom drain blockages — hair, soap, and scale
  • Toilet blockages — foreign objects, excessive paper, root ingress
  • Outside gully and yard drain blockages
  • Main sewer and soil stack blockages
  • Underground drain blockages caused by root intrusion or collapse
  • Shared drain and manhole blockages

CCTV drain surveys

For recurring blockages or drains that aren't clearing fully, a CCTV drain survey tells you exactly what's happening inside the pipe. We pass a waterproof camera through the drain and provide a live video feed, a written report, and a recording.

A CCTV survey is also essential when buying a property, if you're experiencing subsidence, or if your insurer requires a drain condition report before accepting a claim.

Preventing future blockages

Once your drain is clear, we'll advise on what caused the blockage and how to prevent a recurrence. For properties with repeated drain issues, we offer a scheduled jetting programme to keep drains clear year-round.
