24/7 emergency plumbing across Greater Manchester

When a pipe bursts at 2am or your bathroom floods on a Sunday, you need a plumber who actually picks up the phone. Premier Plumbing Co. operates a genuine 24-hour emergency service — not an answerphone that books you in for next Tuesday. Call us any time and we'll dispatch the nearest available Gas Safe engineer immediately.

We cover the whole of Greater Manchester, including Manchester city centre, Salford, Trafford, Stockport, Bury, Oldham, Rochdale, and Wigan. Our average emergency response time is under 60 minutes.

What we treat as an emergency

  • Burst or split pipes — especially if water is spreading through walls or floors
  • Flooding from a broken appliance, failed joint, or blocked drain
  • Loss of water supply — failed stopcock or mains pressure issue
  • Sewage backup or blocked toilet that can't be cleared
  • No heating or hot water during cold weather
  • Boiler making unusual noises, leaking, or showing a fault code
  • Suspected gas or CO leak — call 0800 111 999 first, then us

If you're unsure whether your situation counts as an emergency, call us anyway. We'd rather talk you through it and confirm it's minor than have you wait and risk serious water damage or a health hazard.

How our emergency call-out works

Step 1 — Call us

Dial 0161 123 4567. You'll speak to a real person, not an automated system. Tell us your address and what's happening and we'll locate the nearest available engineer.

Step 2 — Engineer dispatched

We aim to have an engineer at your door within 60 minutes. You'll receive a call from the engineer when they're on their way so you know exactly when to expect them.

Step 3 — Problem assessed and fixed

Our vans are stocked with the most common parts — pipe fittings, joints, stopcocks, pressure vessels — so we can fix most emergency problems on the first visit. We'll give you a fixed price before we start any work.

Pricing

We believe in transparent pricing. There are no hidden fees — you'll know the full cost before we start. Emergency call-out rates are as follows:

  • Weekdays 7am–8pm: no call-out fee, labour charged at standard rate
  • Evenings, weekends & bank holidays: flat £49 call-out fee + labour
  • All parts charged at cost — we don't mark up parts
