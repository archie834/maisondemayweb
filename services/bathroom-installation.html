Bathroom installation across Greater Manchester

Whether you're fitting a new family bathroom, converting a spare room into an en-suite, or installing a level-access wet room, Premier Plumbing Co. handles the complete installation — from removing the old suite to final connections and commissioning.

We offer both a full supply-and-fit service (we source and supply all fixtures, fittings, and materials) and a fit-only service if you've already purchased your bathroom suite. We work to fixed prices agreed before we start — no surprises on the final invoice.

What we install

  • Full bathroom refits — bath, basin, toilet, shower, tiling
  • En-suite bathroom conversions from bedroom space
  • Wet room installation including tanking and drainage
  • Shower enclosures and walk-in shower trays
  • Electric and thermostatic shower fitting
  • Toilet replacement — close-coupled, back-to-wall, and wall-hung
  • Basin and vanity unit fitting
  • Heated towel rail and bathroom radiator installation
  • Underfloor heating under tiled bathroom floors

How it works

Free site visit — We'll visit your property to assess the existing plumbing layout, drainage, and any structural considerations before providing a quote.

Fixed written quote — You'll receive a detailed written quote covering labour, materials, waste disposal, and any associated building work. No day-rate guesswork.

Installation — Most standard bathroom refits take 3–5 days. We protect surrounding areas with dust sheets and clean up fully at the end of each day.

Sign-off — We won't leave until you're happy. All work is guaranteed, and we'll provide a completion certificate for any notifiable work under Part P of the Building Regulations.

Pricing

Bathroom installation costs vary depending on the scope of the project, the fixtures chosen, and any structural or tiling work involved. We give fixed written quotes after a site visit — not rough estimates over the phone. Call us to arrange a free survey.
